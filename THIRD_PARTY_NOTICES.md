## David W. Skinner — Microban I

- Included material: the complete 155-level Microban I collection, in its
  original order.
- Author: David W. Skinner.
- Author contact published with the collection: `sasquatch@bentonrea.com`.
- Unchanged source collection:
  `games/dragoban/third-party-levels/david-w-skinner/microban.txt`.
- Published redistribution condition: Skinner's former site stated that his
  sets may be freely distributed if they remain unchanged and retain his name
  and email address. The original page is no longer online; the statement is
  preserved in a 2003
  [GNU mailing-list discussion](https://lists.gnu.org/archive/html/enigma-devel/2003-12/msg00018.html)

The runtime table is generated from the unchanged collection by
`scripts/generate_microban_levels.py`. It preserves every wall, player, box,
and goal, adding only empty exterior storage cells around smaller maps.
